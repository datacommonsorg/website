Climate change

Max projected summer temperatures for US counties (RCP 4.5) Climate change is not just about reducing carbon emissions. It is also about adaptation to the change that is already happening. The change in temperature is not as simple as 1.5 vs 2 vs 2.5 degrees centigrade. These are global averages. At every one of those levels, there will be places that become much hotter and places that become colder.

Explore what temperatures in the US might be in 2050 (optimistic, pessimistic), in Europe (optimistic, pessimistic). See the progression in Europe over time (2040, 2045, 2050).
