We would love to get your feedback!

Data Issues

If you noticed some error in the data or something that looked odd, you can

  • Send us an email to support at datacommons dot org, or
  • File an issue on Github.

Please make sure you include enough details, including a url or screenshot of the issue.

Contributing Data

We are actively looking to partner with Government organizations, Universities and NGOs to add authoritative data. If you are interested, please email us at collaborations at datacommons dot org

Other Participation

Data Commons has benefited greatly from our collaborations with different government organizations and academic institutions and are looking to expand the set of collaborative projects. In particular, we are looking for partners for creating new tools, building curriculum using Data Commons, for improving documentation and more. Click here for more details.
