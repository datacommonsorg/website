About the Health Data Commons

This Health Data Commons brings together key health and well-being statistics from trusted public sources into one place. The goal is to make it easier to explore trends, compare regions, and understand how health outcomes and determinants vary across places and over time.

What You Can Do Here

You can browse and compare indicators such as life expectancy, disease prevalence, health care access, environmental health factors, and other metrics. Visualizations and tables help you see patterns by geography and year, and you can download data for further analysis or reporting.

Data & Transparency

We rely on official and peer-reviewed datasets from national and international agencies. Each statistic is linked to its source where possible, so you can trace definitions and methods. We do not alter the underlying valuesâ€”only normalize and present them for consistent comparison.

Our Mission

We aim to make public health data more accessible and usable for researchers, policymakers, educators, and the general public, so that data-driven decisions can help improve health outcomes everywhere.
