An open knowledge repository for sustainability

Facing the climate crisis requires a deep understanding of not just the climate, but the various stresses that will impact access to food, shelter, healthcare and other aspects of life. Unfortunately, this data is fragmented across thousands of organizations, in a cacophony of schemas and a multitude of databases, making it very expensive, if not impossible to use.

Google has ‘organized and made easily accessible’ many kinds of information. One of our contributions to tackling the climate crisis is to organize climate and related data and make it easily accessible to consumers, journalists, policy makers and researchers through Sustainability Data Commons. Given the significance of the climate problem, we are doing this in a collaborative, open fashion (like Tensor flow, Kubernetes, etc.) together with Stanford, Harvard and others.
